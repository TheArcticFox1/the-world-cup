The Jules Rimet Trophy

The World Cup took place in Uruguay from the 13th July to 30 July 1930

The first ever FIFA World Cup final took place in Uruguay, on this day 93 years ago, on the 30th July 1930

Uruguay was selected as the host nation in celebration of the country's 100 year anniversary of its 1st Constitution All matches were played in the Uruguayan capital, Montevideo.

The final was played between Uruguay and Argentina where the Hosts won by 4 goals to 2
